About Our Heart Disease Prediction System

Heart disease is one of the leading causes of death globally. Early detection and prevention can significantly reduce risks and save lives.

Our system leverages machine learning algorithms to analyze various health parameters and predict the likelihood of developing heart disease. The model has been trained on real medical data and provides high accuracy in predictions.

Key Features:
  • High prediction accuracy using Gradient Boosting Algorithm
  • Secure storage of your health records
  • Connect with specialized doctors based on results
  • Track your heart health over time

Health Parameters We Analyze

Our system takes into account these important health indicators

Age

Age is a significant risk factor for heart disease. The risk increases as you get older.

Sex

Men are generally at higher risk of heart disease than women, especially at younger ages.

Chest Pain Type

Different types of chest pain (angina) can indicate various heart conditions.

Blood Pressure

High blood pressure (hypertension) is a major risk factor for heart disease.

Cholesterol

High cholesterol levels can lead to plaque buildup in arteries, increasing heart disease risk.

Fasting Blood Sugar

Elevated blood sugar can indicate diabetes, which increases heart disease risk.
