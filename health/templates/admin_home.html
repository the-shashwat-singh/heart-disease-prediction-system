Admin Guide

As an administrator, you have access to manage all aspects of the Heart Disease Prediction System:

  • Manage Doctors: Add, edit, or remove doctors from the system.
  • Manage Patients: View and manage patient accounts and their data.
  • View Predictions: Access all heart disease predictions made by the system.
  • Review Feedback: Check feedback submitted by users to improve the system.
  • System Maintenance: Ensure all components are working properly.

For technical support or questions, please contact the system administrator.
