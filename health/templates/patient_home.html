Heart Health Tips
  • Regular Exercise

    Aim for at least 30 minutes of moderate activity daily, such as brisk walking, swimming, or cycling.

  • Healthy Diet

    Consume a diet rich in fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins. Limit sodium, saturated fats, and sugar.

  • Control Blood Pressure

    Monitor your blood pressure regularly and maintain it within a healthy range to reduce heart disease risk.

  • Manage Stress

    Practice stress-reduction techniques like meditation, deep breathing, or yoga to support heart health.
